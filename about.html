About Crepello

Creplo Restaurant offers many dishes and delicacies such as pizza, various sandwiches, pasta in various shapes and sauces, in addition to fresh salads. It mainly specializes in distinctive Western desserts, such as various pancakes. It is characterized by its comfortable seating and modern and simple design at the same time. If you are in or near Ramallah, do not miss visiting this amazing restaurant.
